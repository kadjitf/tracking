📌 Pet Travel Tips (Preview Section)

Tip #1: Always feed your pet at least 4–6 hours before travel to avoid motion sickness.
👜 Tip #2: Include a favorite blanket or toy in the crate to provide comfort.
💉 Tip #3: Ensure your pet is up-to-date on vaccines and health certificates.
🚫 Tip #4: Avoid sedating your pet unless instructed by a vet.
